It was {new Date().getFullYear() - 2019} years ago. I was 15, browsing Twitch, when the algorithm recommended a stream. [Nick Mira](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC0__rMNmefKfxeB-82fuCWA), making beats on [FL Studio](https://www.image-line.com/flstudio/). I was hooked. I spent hours watching him make beats, learning from him, and trying to make beats myself.

I directly downloaded legally (_ahem_) FL Studio 12, and started making beats. I didn't know what I was doing, but I was having fun. I was learning, and I was creating.
