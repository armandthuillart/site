# Understanding AI

<p className='text-muted-foreground'>January 2026 - Armand Thuillart</p>

I wanted to better understand how AI models are created. Not to become an expert, but to gain an appreciation for the abstractions I use every day. 

This post will highlight what I’ve learned so far. It’s written for other enthusiasts who are new to topics like artificial intelligence, large language models, and the basics of prompting.
